Our History

OceanGuard Collective was founded in 2019 by John Cena with the mission of protecting marine life and keeping our oceans clean. What began as a small-scale beach cleanup initiative has grown into a national movement focused on education, sustainability, and eco-product development.

Why Choose Us

  • We fund local and global ocean cleanup operations
  • Our products are made from ocean-recovered plastic
  • Our programs educate kids and adults about protecting marine ecosystems
  • We are carbon-neutral and partner with green shipping providers
